Silly Orange

Fan page

Orange cats, also known as ginger or marmalade cats, are tabby cats with orange fur due to a specific pigment called pheomelanin. They are commonly, but not exclusively, male, as the gene for orange fur is carried on the X chromosome. They are often known for being friendly and affectionate.
